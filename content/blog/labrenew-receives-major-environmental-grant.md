Cell Signaling Technology has awarded LabRenew significant funding to advance sustainable laboratory practices. This grant will enable LabRenew to expand our mission of transforming how laboratories approach environmental responsibility.

The funding supports initiatives focused on waste reduction, energy efficiency, and green chemistry implementation within scientific research settings.

## About the 2025 Environmental Grant Program

Cell Signaling Technology's program identifies nonprofits demonstrating innovative environmental solutions. The initiative recognizes organizations addressing ecological challenges while creating community-level positive outcomes.

## Looking Forward

LabReNew plans to use the funding for program expansion, educational outreach, and measurable environmental impact creation over the coming year. The organization credits both Cell Signaling Technology and Krystyna Hincman, highlighting how corporate social responsibility can have a meaningful impact on our communities and planet.
